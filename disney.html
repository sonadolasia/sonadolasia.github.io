The Walt Disney Company 

Backend Developer Internship: May - August 2018

In the summer of 2018, I had the opportunity to intern at The Walt Disney Company in Seattle. The goal of my project was to develop a tool, named Hydra, simulating millions of connections to the websocket platform. Hydra is currently being used to ensure that the plaform can scale to expected traffic for customers like ESPN and ABC, wesbites supporting over 100 million daily uniques. 

Hydra was built in Java using the Spring Framework. It was designed to be run in an autoscale group, through AWS, with mutiple instances of Hydra running at once for scale. The Pub/Sub infrastucture of the tool, also allows all instances to be controlled at once.
