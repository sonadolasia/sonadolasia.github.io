the short story
a 6 minute abridged case study
context

While working as project partners in CS 188, Ivy and I had trouble finding a platform where we could collaboratively work together.

Without both of us being able to manipulate and view each other's code, our understanding of the material was unequal.

We tried several different online platforms advertising collaborative coding, but each was missing features we desired.

After attempting to use Google Docs, we started imagining what a collaborative code editor might look like if it was a part of the Google Drive suite.

research

we began by brainstorming a list of features we wanted in a collaborative code editor, and then compared it with similar exisiting products.

research questions

we compiled a question guide for both students and educators to learn more about the role of collaboration in cs eduacation. 

cs students 
  • how have you worked on group CS projects in the past?
  • what tools do you use when coding with others?
  • do you feel that work is equally shared when using those tools?
  • how is your understanding of CS material affected by working with others?
cs educators 
  • how do you explain CS concepts to students?
  • for group projects, how do you judge whether everyone has done equal work?
  • what value does collaboration play in CS education?
