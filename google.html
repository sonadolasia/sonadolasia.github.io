research questions

we compiled a question guide for both students and educators to learn more about the role of collaboration in cs eduacation. 

cs students 
  • how have you worked on group CS projects in the past?
  • what tools do you use when coding with others?
  • do you feel that work is equally shared when using those tools?
  • how is your understanding of CS material affected by working with others?
cs educators 
  • how do you explain CS concepts to students?
  • for group projects, how do you judge whether everyone has done equal work?
  • what value does collaboration play in CS education?
